Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

Welcome to Ahsan Ullah Food Point ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website or place an order through WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and delivery address provided voluntarily when placing an order.
  • Order Information: Items ordered, quantities, and total amounts for fulfilling your order.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our website (collected anonymously via browser).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your food orders accurately.
  • To contact you about your order status via WhatsApp or phone.
  • To respond to your inquiries and feedback.
  • To improve our menu, service quality, and website experience.
  • To send promotional offers (only if you have subscribed).

3. WhatsApp Communication

When you place an order via WhatsApp, your phone number, name, and delivery details are shared directly through WhatsApp's platform. This communication is subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy. We do not store WhatsApp messages on our servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only:

  • With delivery staff solely to complete your order.
  • When required by law or government authority.

5. Data Storage & Security

Order information shared via WhatsApp is stored within WhatsApp's secure infrastructure. Any contact details you provide via our website contact form are used only for responding to you and are not stored in any external database.

6. Cookies

Our website uses the browser's localStorage to save your cart items between page visits. No third-party tracking cookies are used. You can clear your cart data at any time by clicking "Clear Cart" on the cart page or by clearing your browser's local storage.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about what personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of receiving promotional messages at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp at +92 346 7407813.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Phone / WhatsApp: +92 346 7407813
  • Address: Main Bazar, Okara, Punjab, Pakistan
  • Hours: Daily 12 PM – 12 AM
